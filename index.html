Honest answers

The questions everyone asks first

Short answers here — deeper, plain-English answers on our Learn page.

What is AI, in plain English?

AI is software that can read, write, listen, and make simple decisions — the kinds of tasks that used to need a person. In a business, that means it can answer customer emails, summarize documents, fill in paperwork, and keep track of details, so you and your team spend time on work that actually needs a human.

Do I need to be technical to work with Koken Labs?

No. Most of our clients aren't technical at all. We explain everything in plain English, we handle the technical work ourselves, and you never have to touch code. If you can describe how your business works, that's all we need.

How much does it cost to get started with AI?

Less than most people expect. Many useful AI tools cost $20–$60 per person per month. The discovery call is free, training workshops start in the hundreds, and a typical done-for-you setup is a fixed project price we agree on before any work starts. You'll always know the number up front.

Will AI replace my staff?

In our experience, no — it takes over the repetitive parts of their jobs, like retyping information, sorting messages, and writing first drafts. Your people keep the judgment calls and the relationships. Most teams end up doing more valuable work, not less work.

What happens on the free discovery call?

We talk for 30 minutes about how your business runs and where things feel slow or messy. Then we tell you honestly whether AI can help, roughly what it would cost, and what we'd do first. No tech knowledge needed, no pressure, and no obligation — you leave with clarity either way.
