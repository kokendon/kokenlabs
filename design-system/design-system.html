05 · The ō mark

Logo rules

DO  render in the Nova gradient · default to the dark presentation · keep clear space = height of the ō · monogram only below 32px.

DON'T  recolor outside the gradient · add glows / bevels / shadows · stretch, rotate, or close the ring gap · place on a busy photo without a dark scrim.

The open ring is the whole idea — chaos with one clean gap. Never close it.
